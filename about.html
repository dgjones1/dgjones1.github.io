about me

Hi! My name is Dory and I am a Meteorologist on the journey to becoming a Data Scientist. I decided to change career paths so that I can pursure my interests in analyzing data and computer programming.

In May 2022, I graduated from North Carolina State University with a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology and minors in Environmental Science and Geology. Currently, I am a student at Wake Tech in the Data Science and Programming Support Services program. Coursework from my time at NC State included Calculus, Differential Equations, Physics, Statistics, and a host of Atmospheric Science courses. Some of my future coursework at Wake Tech will include Python, Database Programming, Data Structure and Algorithms, and Software Development.
