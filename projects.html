projects

  • Relationship Between El Nino-Southern Oscillation and Wildfires in the Western United States, Spring 2022: I analyzed drought conditions in the western US during the 2010-2011 La Nina and 2015-2016 El Nino events. Using GrADS software, I plotted temperature and precipitation anomalies. An example of one of my plots can be seen on the right.
  • Effect of Climate Change on Tropical Cyclone Translation Speed, Spring 2022: I plotted and analyzed tropical cyclone translation speed data over the last century and discussed possible impacts on storm surge and flooding along the NC coast.
  • Remnants of Hurricane Ida - Tornado Outbreak Case Study, Spring 2022: I studied the environmental conditions that supported the development of a tornadic supercell in Mullica Hill, NJ. Using Python code, I plotted radar reflectivity and radial velocity.
  • Micrometeorological Fluxes at North Carolina Clearcut Site, Spring 2022: I analyzed energy balance, flux footprint dispersion and seasonal changes in carbon dioxide and latent heat fluxes at NC clearcut (US-NC1) site. My energy balance plot can be seen on the right.
